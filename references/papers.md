### LightGBM: A Highly Efficient Gradient Boosting Decision Tree

- Authors: Ke et al.
- Year: 2017
- Venue: NeurIPS
- Link: https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper/2017/hash/6449f44a102fde848669bdd9eb6b76fa-Abstract.html
